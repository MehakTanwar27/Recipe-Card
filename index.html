Delicious Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken pieces
  • 1 cup Fried Onions
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Coriander and Mint Leaves
  • Saffron soaked in Milk

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, lemon juice, and ginger-garlic paste for at least 2 hours.
  2. Cook rice until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Layer the marinated chicken and partially cooked rice in a pot. Sprinkle fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk on top.
  4. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook on a low flame for 40-50 minutes.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes after cooking, then mix gently. Serve hot.

Serving Size: 4 people
